 ###### March 13 
 
 ### National Emergency Declared

 ###### March 13

### Travel Ban

The US government issues a Travel Ban on Non-US citizens traveling from Europe [[1]](https://www.ajmc.com/view/a-timeline-of-covid19-developments-in-2020).
